|culture= Mesoamerican}The Lenca led by Lempira are a custom civilization mod by Tradboy, with contributions from DarthKyofu and NopeCopter.

This mod requires Brave New World.

== Overview ==
=== '''Dawn of Man''' ===
=
Blessings to you Lempira, Lord of the Hills of Cerquín and military leader of the Lenca, the ferocious Jaguar people of El Salvador and Honduras. Your people lived alongside the Maya and Pipiles, thriving among them as great builders of pyramids and fierce warriors. Under your command, you unified the Lencas, creating a military force to push back against the Spanish, with poisonous spears on hand and deep knowledge of the mountain ranges.
Great hero of Central America, will you sharpen your arrows and poison the tips of your spears to prove to the world stage that the Jaguar people will not be forgotten. Can you build a civilization that will stand the test of time?
Introduction:  You've entered the territory of the Jaguar People, I, Lempira, recognize you're bravery for stepping towards us,beware not to cross me.
Defeat: You’ve bested the people of the Jaguar, but you will never be as fierce as my people are, so keep in mind that you will never be the Jaguar, just the lucky hand stand stumble to it's heart!
